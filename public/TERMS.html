5. Privacy and Data Security

5.1 Encryption and Security

  • Your personal content (journals, goals, personal data) is encrypted end-to-end on your device before storage
  • Only you hold the decryption keys for your encrypted content
  • PDF embeddings are stored unencrypted to enable RAG functionality but cannot be reversed to original text
  • User-to-document mappings are encrypted to protect privacy

5.2 AI and Third-Party Service Integration

When you use AI features (Roadmap, Journal assistance, Reading Reflection), the following occurs:

  1. Relevant content is decrypted locally on your device
  2. Decrypted data is securely transmitted to our server via TLS encryption
  3. Our server forwards the data to appropriate AI providers (Perplexity, Anthropic Claude, Amazon Titan) without storing or logging it
  4. Responses are relayed back through the same secure process

5.3 Calendar Integration
